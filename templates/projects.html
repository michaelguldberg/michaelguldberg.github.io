Wellness Tracking | 2022 Baseball Season

In this project, I used a multitude of features to track my overall wellness during the 2022 MiLB season in Midland, TX. I then applied Random Forest Regression and Multivariate Linear Regression to predict my Whoop recovery score as well as assess feature importances. Important variables which had a positive relationship with my recovery were HRV, Hours of Sleep, Day of Week, Hydration, and Overall Positivity Score. Features with a negative relationship with recovery included Alcohol (# of drinks), Sick Feeling Score, and Soreness / Fatigue Score.
